Many families and communities are already worried about the physical health of their loved ones,
and depression intensifies health issues, including increased risk of cardiac mortality.

Love heals.


The Vytality web app gives you ideas.
From the best and worst places to take your loved one,
to suggested activities and conversation prompts that can help improve mood or at least get them out of the hole.
Learn about the best places to live more happily and independently, based on level of depression.

Be a hero in someone's health story.


The Vytality mobile app has a unique, patented ability to pinpoint,
based on mobile sensors and app generated data, who in the community
may be suffering from depression and/or at risk of heart attack.
It provides options on how to help or get help,
with local resources to prompt ideas for activities and conversation (via census, Yelp and mental health resources).
