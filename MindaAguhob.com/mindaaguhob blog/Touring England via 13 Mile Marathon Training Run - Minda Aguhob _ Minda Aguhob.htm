Touring England via 13 Mile Marathon Training Run

I flew to London for an English wedding in the countryside to celebrate the union of my cousin and her Brit man. Mr. & Ms. Nick and Jo Adams = Dance partaay in Castlecombe!

After this, I head straight to Nevada for Burning Man.

My first non-NYC burner friend (burner=Burning Man participant/enthusiast), Dave, or Daithi, “Daw-he, ”as I like to call him, is an ultramarathoner from London. He mapped a training run out for me, and I ran it all. First stop of the 13.5 mile training run / tour of London, was Primrose Hill.

I found this quote, etched in stone on the peak of the hill: “I have conversation with the spiritual sun. I saw him on primrose hill.” William Blake 1757-1827.
